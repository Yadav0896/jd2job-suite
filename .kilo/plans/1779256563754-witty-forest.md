# Plan: Interview Copilot → AI Copilot Platform (Multi-Vertical Expansion)

## Executive Summary

Rebrand and expand "Interview Copilot" → **AI Copilot** — a unified real-time AI coaching platform serving three high-value verticals simultaneously: Interview Intelligence, Sales Intelligence, and Meeting Intelligence. All three share the same technical foundation (real-time audio → STT → LLM → real-time suggestions) but differ in objectives, personas, and output formats.

**Key Decision:** Build all 3 verticals simultaneously on shared infrastructure (sidebar + platform shell first, then all 3 panels in parallel).

**Key Decision for Sales:** Replace CRM integration with a **Pre-Call Research Agent** — user provides client name, role, and company details before the call; AI does deep company research and generates a briefing document, objection predictions, and discovery questions.

## Implementation Plan

**Goal:** Unify the three verticals into one app with shared infrastructure. Rename to "AI Copilot".

1. **Rename product** — "Interview Copilot" → "AI Copilot" across all files, titles, CSS
2. **Sidebar Component (`Sidebar.jsx`)** — vertical switcher with icons, status indicators, active state
3. **Platform Router** — `platformMode: 'interview' | 'sales' | 'meeting'` in AppContext drives which panels render

5. **Credits System** — Unify pricing across verticals (credit-based: 1 credit = 1 hour of coaching)
6. **Session History** — Cross-vertical history with playback capability
7. **`platformPromptBuilder` service** — Shared LLM infrastructure dispatching to vertical-specific prompt builders

### Phase 2: All Three Verticals Simultaneously (Week 3-5)

**Goal:** Build interview (refactor existing), sales, and meeting panels in parallel on the shared infrastructure.

#### 2A: Refactor Interview Mode (already built, needs cleanup)
- Extract existing interview logic into `InterviewPanel.jsx` wrapper
- Keep all existing features (ghost mode, keyword tracker, screen reader)
- Add pre-interview briefing modal (already have Resume + JD inputs)

#### 2B: Sales Intelligence

**2B-1: Pre-Call Research Agent** (unique differentiator — no competitor does this)

```
User inputs:
  - Client name: "Sarah Chen"
  - Client role: "VP of Engineering"  
  - Company: "Stripe"
  - Deal context: "Selling our payment fraud detection API"
  - Methodology: "MEDDIC"

AI Copilot does deep research:
  1. Company background (Crunchbase, news, funding, size)
  2. Tech stack (BuiltWith, StackShare, job listings)
  3. Recent news (product launches, leadership changes, acquisitions)
  4. Competitive landscape (who else sells to them, what tools they use)
  5. Pain points (from G2 reviews, support forums, Glassdoor)
  6. Key decision criteria (from job descriptions, annual reports)

Output: Briefing Document with:
  - Executive summary of the company
  - Sarah's likely priorities (based on VP Eng role)
  - Top 3 potential objections + counter-arguments
  - 10 discovery questions tailored to Stripe
  - Suggested ROI metrics to highlight
  - Competitor intel (who they compare against)
  - Talk track for opening 2 minutes
```

**Implementation:**
1. **SalesBriefingModal.jsx** — Pre-call input: client name, role, company, deal context, methodology
2. **SalesResearchAgent.js (`services/salesResearchService.js`)** — Calls NVIDIA LLM with web search context to generate briefing
3. **SalesBriefingCard.jsx** — Displays briefing document in sidebar/before call starts

**2B-2: Sales Call Coaching** (live during call)
1. **SalesContext.jsx** — Sales-specific state (objections, signals, methodology, briefing data)
2. **SalesAnswerPanel.jsx** — Modified AnswerPanel with:
   - Objection handling responses (powered by pre-call research context)
   - Discovery question prompts
   - Competitive battle cards
   - Pricing/negotiation tips
   - "Next best action" suggestions
3. **SalesPromptBuilder (`services/salesService.js`)** — LLM prompt engineering for:
   - `[OBJECTION_DETECTED]` → 3-tier response (counter, reframe, defer) with pre-call research context
   - `[BUYING_SIGNAL]` → suggest demo, trial, reference call, ROI proof
   - `[COMPETITOR_MENTIONED]` → battle card with specific differentiators from research
   - `[PRICING_DISCUSSION]` → negotiation tactics with industry benchmarks
   - `[NEXT_STEPS]` → action items + CRM notes (ready to paste)
5. **Talk Ratio Monitor** — Visual indicator of rep vs prospect speaking time
6. **Objection Tracker (`ObjectionTracker.jsx`)** — Real-time: which objections surfaced, which were handled

**Prompt engineering with pre-call research:**
```
System: You are an expert sales coach using {methodology} methodology.
Pre-call research on {company}:
  - Founded {year}, {size} employees, {funding}
  - Tech stack: {stack}
  - Recent news: {news}
  - Likely pain points: {pain_points}
  - Key decision makers: {dm_info}

Client context: {client_name}, {role} at {company}
Deal context: {deal_context}

[OBJECTION] → Provide 3 response options using company-specific context
[BUYING_SIGNAL] → Suggest next action aligned with their buying process
[COMPETITOR] → Provide 2 differentiators based on competitor analysis from research
```

#### 2C: Meeting Intelligence

1. **MeetingContext.jsx** — Meeting-specific state (action items, decisions, gaps, agenda)

6. **Decision Log (`DecisionLog.jsx`)** — Running decision record with timestamps
